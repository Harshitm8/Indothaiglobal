About Indo Thai Global

Indo Thai Global is engaged in manufacturing and supplying innovative solar drying solutions and food processing equipment since 2005. Our systems are designed to provide efficient and hygienic drying for agricultural and industrial applications across India and beyond.

We focus on sustainability, energy efficiency and long-term durability. Our equipment is used by farmers, food processing industries and entrepreneurs looking to reduce post-harvest loss and increase productivity.

Established 2005
Location Ludhiana, Punjab, India
Speciality Solar dryers & food processing equipment
Industries Agriculture · Food Processing · Pharma

Our Expertise

We manufacture solar dryers, hybrid dryers and agricultural processing machines designed for commercial and small-scale applications.
